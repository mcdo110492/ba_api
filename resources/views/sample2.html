Special Large truck/vendor delivery directions

Trucks should enter via the main gate on 140th Ave. NE. If your arrival is after hours you may find parking across the street to the west of the Skate King Parking lot. Call the store and ask for the yard foreman or send a message for further information concerning deliveries.
